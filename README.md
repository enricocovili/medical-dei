
### Enhancing the Quality of Orthopanoramic and Teleradiography Images through Anonymization and Alignment Correction

---

#### Description
This thesis will explore methods for the anonymization and alignment correction of teleradiography and orthopanoramic images, part of a significant dataset being collected in collaboration with the University of Ferrara. 
Due to the nature of their acquisition, whereby some images were digitized by photographing physical scans, there exists a challenge in maintaining the images' alignment, which is crucial for accurate analysis and diagnostic purposes. 
Moreover, the requirement for anonymization of these images before public release adds an additional layer of complexity, necessitating the development of effective techniques that safeguard patient privacy without compromising the integrity of the image data. 
This research aims to address these challenges by developing a comprehensive framework that incorporates image processing and possibly machine learning algorithms to correct alignment issues and ensure anonymization, thereby enhancing the usability of this dataset for medical research and diagnostic applications.
